Privacy Policy for KasirHPP

Last Updated: May 29, 2026

Welcome to KasirHPP. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile point-of-sale (cashier) application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a functional, secure, and synchronized experience for managing your business, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Identity Information: Email address and password (used strictly for account creation and authentication via Firebase Auth).
  • Business and Transaction Data: Product names, prices, Cost of Goods Sold (HPP), stock levels, sales transaction history, and customer notes that you input into the application. This data is stored securely in Cloud Firestore.
  • Media and Files (Product Images): Images you upload from your device's gallery or camera to be used as product photos. These images are processed and stored securely in our cloud storage linked to your account.
  • Device Information: Unique device identifiers and basic system logs for app stability, synchronization, and security purposes.

2. Device Permissions

To support the product image upload feature, the app will request the following permissions:

  • Storage / Photo Gallery Access: Required to select existing product images from your device.
  • Camera Access (Optional): Required if you choose to take a photo of a product directly through the app.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To identify you as a business owner and allow secure access to your data across devices.
  • App Functionality: To calculate HPP, track sales, display product images, and manage your cashier operations.
  • Data Synchronization: To sync your business records continuously via our cloud database.

4. Third-Party Services

Our application uses services provided by Google to ensure high security and infrastructure reliability:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore & Firebase Storage

These third parties may access your data only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose. You can view Google’s Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Retention: We retain your business data and product images as long as your account is active.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated business data. You can do this through the settings menu in the app or by contacting us at sakuduang.coffee@gmail.com. Upon deletion, all your records in Firestore, product images in Firebase Storage, and authentication logs will be permanently deleted.

6. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your business records. All communication between KasirHPP and Firebase is encrypted via HTTPS/SSL. Passwords are fully encrypted by Firebase Authentication, and we do not have access to your raw password.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
